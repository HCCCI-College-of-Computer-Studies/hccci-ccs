Our College develops skilled, creative, and globally competitive technology professionals, equipping students with the technical expertise, problem-solving abilities, and industry-relevant knowledge needed in today's digital era.

We offer a wide range of programs — BSIT, BSIS, BSCS, and the two-year ACT program — providing pathways for diverse career goals in the tech industry. With hands-on trainings, real-world projects, and holistic development, graduates are well-prepared to thrive in the fast-evolving world of technology.

BSIS
Bachelor of Science in
Information Systems

Merges business management with IT, designing and implementing solutions for organizational challenges. Prepares students for systems analysis, data analysis, and project management.

Career Pathways
Data AnalystSystem AnalystIS Project ManagerApp DeveloperQA SpecialistSolution SpecialistProcess AnalystEnd User Trainer
4-Year Program
BSCS
Bachelor of Science in
Computer Science

Focuses on theoretical foundations, algorithms, and computing applications including software engineering and system design. Math-intensive — calculus, linear algebra, discrete mathematics.

Career Pathways
Software EngineerSystem DeveloperR&D ProfessionalApp DeveloperData AnalystComputer ProgrammerQA SpecialistSoftware Support
2-Year Program
ACT

Computer Technology

A ladderized technical program giving foundational IT skills for programming, networking, and web development. Graduates can enter the workforce immediately or pursue a full BS degree.

What You Can Become

Career Paths.

A CCS degree opens doors across the entire tech industry. From building apps to securing networks — our graduates go on to shape the digital landscape.

Software Developer

Build applications, systems, and software solutions across mobile, web, and enterprise platforms.

Cybersecurity Specialist

Protect systems and networks from digital threats, ensuring data integrity and security compliance.

Data Scientist / Analyst

Extract actionable insights from large datasets and drive data-informed decisions across industries.

AI / ML Engineer

Design intelligent systems leveraging machine learning, deep learning, and AI technologies.

Systems Analyst

Analyze business requirements and design IT solutions that align technology with organizational goals.

Network Engineer

Design, implement, and maintain communication networks for organizations of every scale.
